WHAT’S HOT

Tsimane is located within a National
Park and Indigenous Territory, where
the Amazon jungle meet
the Andes Mountain chain

WHAT’S HOT

Tsimane remains one of the worlds greatest fly fishing adventures!  The fishing is unparalleled, and the people and place cap off an epic adventure

WHAT’S HOT

As a photographer and fisherman I consider my 2 visits to Tsamane to be among the very best experiences in a lifetime of world travel. Great fishing, excellent accommodations with the chance to experience a vanishing way of life are memories for a lifetime.

WHAT’S HOT

I have been fortunate to experience unique places with great fishing but Tsimane left be speechless.  The beauty of the jungle, the challenge of the river and the greatest freshwater gamefish on earth make this adventure one of a kind

WHAT’S HOT

To date, when people ask me what my most memorable destination trip is, I tell them about Bolivia. The culture, the people, the fishing, and the scenery... it is truly one of the last places on earth we can travel to as anglers and still feel as though we're in a world untouched by modern man
